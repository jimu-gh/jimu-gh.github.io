In creating this Augmented Reality game, I wanted to experiment with iOS CoreLocation to make something easy and fun to play while maintaining replay value. In HomeSafe, the player's goal is to reach a target location on the map (by actually walking) without running into randomly generated virtual monsters.
 • Uses live GPS to track user and monster location
 • "Smart" and random monster pathing
 • Conceived and created in a single day
