Kashi, a BTT widget that displays the current song's lyrics in the Macbook Touch Bar, is the result of otaku levels of audiophilicity and too much free time over a single holiday weekend. It gets the current song's artist/song data, intelligently queries and scrapes the Genius API, and outputs the prettified result onto the Touch Bar.

 • Displays current lyrics

 • Compatible with Spotify and iTunes

 • ~100 lines of Python and Applescript
